Commission Pricing Information

Below you can find a rough breakdown of how much something would cost

The final price will depend on a few factors, and will be provided before work on a piece starts. The desired content or level of quality can be adjusted during work on the piece, however if work was already put into an aspect that was adjusted, the cost for it will be partially included.

Lines

    Breakdown

  • Sketch - 15€
  • Solid Lines - 30€
  • Inked Lines - 40€

Color

    Breakdown

  • No color - 0
  • Monochrome - 5€
  • Flat - 10€
  • Simple Tones - 15€
  • Complex Tones - 20€

Shading

    Breakdown

  • No Shading - 0€
  • Basic Shading - 10€
  • Complex Shading - 15€
  • Smooth Shading - 20€

Characters

    Breakdown

  • Main Subject - 0€
  • Additional Subjects - 50% of Cost
  • Complex Patterns/Body - Depends on Complexity and Specificity
  • Outfits/Objects - Depends on Level of Detail

Extras

    Breakdown

  • Multiple Angles/Views - Depends on Complexity compared to base scene
  • Alts/Edits - May incur costs if requires significant adjustments
  • Backgrounds - May incur costs if is complex or detailed
  • NSFW - 10€

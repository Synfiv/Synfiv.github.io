Welcome to my website

Hi, I am Saru. I am an artist in the process of expanding my hobby.

I work mostly with 2D digital art ranging from sketches to drawings and painting, and am experienced with 3D animation as-well as learning modelling. The focus of my art has thus far been on characters, and I have done some work with landscapes and objects.

If my work catches your interest, I am open to commissions, details and contact information can be found in this website.
